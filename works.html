About Me

I’m a data analyst with a strong foundation in mathematics and a passion for transforming data into meaningful insights. With experience in analyzing sales trends, forecasting demand, and supporting inventory planning, I use SQL, Excel, Google Sheets, and Tableau to uncover patterns that drive smarter business decisions.

My journey into data analytics began with a deep love for problem-solving and numbers. I earned my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Philippine Normal University in 2008, which shaped my analytical thinking and attention to detail. Now, I’m excited to apply my skills to real-world challenges—exploring trends, building models, and continuously learning new ways to turn raw data into valuable insights.
