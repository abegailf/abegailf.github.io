Project Background:

I designed this case study as a way to show off my Google Analytics 4 (GA4) skills. I used a demo version of the Google Merch Shopâ€™s data, which has some limitations in terms of access and features. To make this project more realistic, I came up with some pretend objectives that a sales and executive team might have for this kind of e-commerce business. This gave me a solid framework to analyze the data and demonstrate what I can do.

Objectives for the Sales Team:

  • Identify the most popular products that generate the highest revenue in the past 28 days.
  • Analyze the purchase funnel to identify any bottlenecks or drop-off points.

    • Objectives for the Executive Team:

  • Analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) such as website traffic, conversion rates, and revenue over the last 12 months.
  • Identify trends and patterns in website traffic and sales data to inform business strategy.

    • Some of the things I explored:

  • Top sellers: Which products are bringing in the most revenue?
  • Purchase funnel: Where are people dropping off before completing their orders?
  • Year-over-year trends: How is the shop performing compared to last year?

    • Based on my GA4 analysis, I even came up with some data-driven recommendations for the Google Merch team!
